John Soane

Coming from humble working class origins, John Soane's hard work earned him sponsorship from the Royal Academy to travel the Grand Tour from 1778 to 1780. He was also one of the first to pioneer the usage of Greek Doric columns in public British buildings, which can be exemplified in his work for the Bank of England, as well as public offices in Whitehall and Westminister.
