Robert Adam

Growing up in a family of Scottish and British architects, Robert Adam was no stranger to the then-vogue Palladian style. However, his travels and relationship with Italian printmaker Giovanni Piranesi show a shift towards a combination of both Palladian, and more ornate details in British household interiors.
